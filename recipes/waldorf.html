Waldorf Salad

A fresh Christmas salad

Ingredients

  • 1/2 head of lettuce
  • 1/2 green apple
  • 1/2 red apple
  • 2 celery stalks and celery leaves
  • 1 handful of walnuts

Dressing:

  • 1 tsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 2 tsp plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream (or whipping cream)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Wash and dry the lettuce and celery. Dice the green and red apples (you may choose to peel them first). Finely chop the celery stalks and leaves.
  2. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise (1 tsp), mustard (1 tsp), plain yogurt (2 tsp), and heavy cream (2 tbsp).
  3. Season the Dressing: Add salt and pepper to taste. Whisk until the dressing is smooth and creamy. Drizzle a splash of olive oil and mix again.
  4. Combine Solids: In a large mixing bowl, gently combine the diced apples, chopped celery, and walnuts.
  5. Assemble the Salad: Pour the prepared dressing over the apple, celery, and walnut mixture. Gently toss everything until all ingredients are lightly coated.
  6. Serve: Line your serving plate with the lettuce leaves. Spoon the dressed apple mixture over the lettuce and serve immediately for the freshest taste, or chill briefly before serving.
